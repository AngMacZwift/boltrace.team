Thunder Bolt Stage Race: October 2019

Important Notice

We are aware that a significant number of riders were affected by Zwift dropping out for day 1 of the Thunder Bolt Stage Race. As a contingency, we will be giving all riders who continue the stage race the pack time plus 15 seconds for day 1, to allow riders the opportunity to complete the event.

Welcome

Welcome to the Five Day Thunder Bolt Stage Race, running from October 18th to October 23rd. The stage race uses a time-based General Classification, as well as having a sprinter and climber classification. The sprinter and climber classifications will award the first 6 of each grade points (10, 7, 5, 3, 2, 1) for all sprint and KOM segments in each race. Racers will need to complete at least four events to be counted in the classifications.
