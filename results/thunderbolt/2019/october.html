Thunder Bolt Stage Race: October 2019

Welcome to the Five Day Thunder Bolt Stage Race, running from October 18th to October 23rd. The stage race uses a time-based General Classification, as well as having a sprinter and climber classification. The sprinter and climber classifications will award the first 6 of each grade points (10, 7, 5, 3, 2, 1) for all sprint and KOM segments in each race. Racers will need to complete at least four events to be counted in the classifications. If there are enough numbers, we will have separate classifications for women as well.

Race Schedule

  • October 18, 18:30 UTC
  • October 19, 19:20 UTC
  • October 20, 19:00 UTC
  • October 21, 18:30 UTC
  • October 23, 18:35 UTC

Rules

  • Must be registered with ZwiftPower for official results
  • Heart rate monitors are recommended, but not required
  • Powermeter or Smart Trainer required for official result placement
  • Riders going over category limits will be upgraded
  • Race Commissar’s decision will be final

Categories (best 30 day est. FTP)

  1. 4.0W/kg and OVER
  2. 3.2W/kg to 3.99W/kg
  3. 2.5W/kg to 3.19W/kg
  4. 2.49W/kg and UNDER
