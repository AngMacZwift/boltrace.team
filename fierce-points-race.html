Fierce Felines Points Race Scoring

The finishing order is determind by points that are awarded to the first 6 across the line in each sprint (10, 7, 5, 3, 2, 1), with double points for the finish. Ties are broken by finish time.

Points for the round use the normal points table.

This is an example of how the results of a points race is determined for scoring in the Fierce Felines events.

And even though Milleu was first across the line, because she didn't place as well in the intermediate sprints, did not win the race. In contrast, with Hamilton winning 3 intermediate sprints, despite crossing the finish line in 4th, still secured 2nd place. Lap 7 was the final lap, so double points were awarded.
