Fierce Felines Racing Series

Event Information

The Fierce Felines Racing Series is a new series created by the BRT Hellcatz, providing hard racing for women across three timezones. We believe that women need more opportunities to race against women, and that races should be as equally hard as some of the top mens racing in Zwift. This series provides races that should cater to all abilities, with time trials, points races, fairly flat road races, and hilly road races.

Our first series will be a shorter format, five weeks, with the grand finale tackling the mighty Alpe du Zwift. We'll start with a flat time trial through the streets of London, a combination of pan flat desert and rolling lush greens in Watopia, stay in Watopia once more for a fast and furious points race around the volcano, the constant rolling flats of New York's Central Park, and then finish with a hill top finish up the mighty Alpe.

We will be using ZwiftPower's womens categories for all races.

Race Rules

  • Women only. Shared accounts not eligible for official results.
  • Must be registered with ZwiftPower.
  • Riders exceeding 5W/kg FTP efforts will be investigated, with possibility for DQ.
  • zPower is not eligible for results.
  • Powerups will be awarded at every banner, 25% possibility of Leaf, Van, Aero and Burrito.
  • Race Commissaire's decision will be final.

Individual Points

  • 100-1 points. All finishers will receive at minimum 1 point.
  • Points scoring applied to each category.
  • For the criterium, finish order will be determined by sprint points (10, 7, 5, 3, 2, 1), and then race points determined from this finish order.

Team Points

  • Each team will score the sum of their top 3 individual rider points.
  • There will be separate podiums for teams in each category.

Points Rules

  • Points can only be earned in the correct category a participant races in.

Mid-League Upgrades

  • Riders going over category limits will be automatically upgraded in the official results.
  • When a rider has been automatically upgraded, they are expected to race in their new category for the remainder of the series.
