BRT Workouts

How To Use

Copy the downloaded workout files to the Zwift/Workouts folder in your Documents folder.

The Workouts

  • The 30

    The 30 has been designed to increase a cyclists VO2 Max using short intensive intervals.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 mins warmup
    • 12x 30secs @ 120% ftp and 12x 30secs @ rest
    • 4 mins @ rest
    • 12x 30secs @ 120% ftp and 12x 30secs @ rest
    • 4 mins @ rest
    • 12x 30secs @ 120% ftp and 12x 30secs @ rest
    • 8 mins cooldown

  • The Alternator

    The Alternator has been designed to target over and unders with a leg snapper at the end of each set

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 minute warmup.
    • 1st set = 2x 2 mins @ 95% ftp, 2x 2 mins @ 105% ftp, 1x 1 min @ 90% and 1x 30 seconds @ 170% ftp.
    • 5 minutes @ rest.
    • 2nd set = 2x 2 mins @ 95% ftp, 2x 2 mins @ 105% ftp, 1x 1min @ 90% and 1x 30 seconds @ 170% ftp.
    • 5 minutes @ rest
    • 3rd set = 2x 2 mins @ 95% ftp, 2x 2 mins @ 105% ftp, 1x 1 min @ 90% ftp and 1x 30 seconds @ 170% ftp.
    • 8 minute cooldown.

  • The Blade

    The Blade has been designed to work on a rider's threshold/VO2 max zone.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 minute warmup.
    • Each set comprises of 60 second blocks @ 120%, 100%, 90%, 100% and 120-140% ftp.
    • 4 minutes @ rest between each set.
    • Final set comprises of 60 second blocks @ 120-130%, 100-110%, 90-100%, 100-110% and 120-160% ftp.
    • 8 minute cooldown.

    • 5 min @ rest
    • 3x 15 seconds@ 150% ftp with 3x 5 min @ 90% ftp
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Punch

    The Punch has been designed to get you race ready for Zwift races. The focus is on sustained 4 minute and 1 minute power surges with an FTP drop off to allow you to settle back into the pack or over the top of a climb.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 4x 4 min @ 125% ftp
    • 30 sec @ 100% ftp
    • 4 min @ rest after each block
    • 3x 1 min @ 150% ftp
    • 30 sec @ 100% ftp
    • 2:30 min @ rest after each block
    • Finishing with an 8 min cool down

    • 6x sprints @ 225% ftp with 60 second rests.
    • 5 minutes @ rest
    • 4x sprints @ 280% ftp with 90 second rests.
