BRT Workouts

How To Use

Copy the downloaded workout files to the Zwift/Workouts folder in your Documents folder.

The Workouts

  • The 30

    The 30 has been designed to increase a cyclists VO2 Max using short intensive intervals.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 mins warmup
    • 12x 30secs @ 120% ftp and 12x 30secs @ rest
    • 4 mins @ rest
    • 12x 30secs @ 120% ftp and 12x 30secs @ rest
    • 4 mins @ rest
    • 12x 30secs @ 120% ftp and 12x 30secs @ rest
    • 8 mins cooldown

  • The Burst

    The Burst has been designed to focus on a cross between sweet spot and neuromuscular training within a 60 minute window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 2 min @ 115-120% ftp
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 3x 15 seconds @ 150% ftp with 3x 5 min @ 90% ftp
    • 5 min @ rest
    • 3x 15 seconds@ 150% ftp with 3x 5 min @ 90% ftp
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Cross

    The Cross workout has been designed to focus on RAB's within an approximate 60 min window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 3 min @ 90-110% ftp
    • 4 min @ rest
    • (4x) 8x 15secs @ 125% ftp and 8x 20secs @ 90% ftp with 4mins rest between each interval set.
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Descent

    The Descent has been designed to focus on sweet spot training within an approximate 60 minute window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 1 min @ 115% ftp
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 6x 1 min @ 90% ftp with 6x 30 second @ rest
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 6x 45 second @ 90% ftp with 6x 30 second @ rest
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 6x 30 second @ 90% ftp with 6x 30 second @ rest
    • 2x 1 min @ 120% ftp with 2x 2min @ rest
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Edge

    The Edge workout has been designed to focus on threshold training within an approximate 60 min window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 5 min @ 100% ftp
    • 5 min @ rest
    • 2x 10 min @ 95-105% ftp with 1x 5 min @ rest
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Goat

    The Goat workout has been designed to focus on hill training within an approximate 60 min window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 8x 1:30 mins @ 140% ftp with 7x @ rest
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Maximus

    The Maximus workout has been designed to focus on VO2 training within an approximate 60 min window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 4 min @ 100% ftp
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 4x 3 min @ 120% with 4x 4 min @ rest
    • 4 min @ 100% ftp
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Micro

    The Micro workout has been designed to focus on neuromuscular training within an approximate 60 min window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 10 min micro burst intervals (15 seconds on @ 150% ftp & 30 second off)
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 10 min micro burst intervals (15 seconds on @ 150% ftp & 30 seconds off)
    • 4 min @ rest
    • 10 min micro burst intervals (15 seconds on @ 150% ftp & 30 seconds off)
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Power Hour

    The Power Hour workout has been designed to focus on pushing a riders limits within an approximate 60 min window.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 10 min @ 90-110% ftp
    • 5 min @ rest
    • 10 min @ 105% ftp
    • 5 min @ rest
    • 10 min @ 90-110% ftp
    • 8 min cool down

  • The Punch

    The Punch has been designed to get you race ready for Zwift races. The focus is on sustained 4 minute and 1 minute power surges with an FTP drop off to allow you to settle back into the pack or over the top of a climb.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warm up
    • 4x 4 min @ 125% ftp
    • 30 sec @ 100% ftp
    • 4 min @ rest after each block
    • 3x 1 min @ 150% ftp
    • 30 sec @ 100% ftp
    • 2:30 min @ rest after each block
    • Finishing with an 8 min cool down

  • The Sickener

    The Sickener has been designed to target a cyclists neuromuscular zone to increase anaerobic capacity. This workout is best carried out on fresh, rested legs.

    Workout Breakdown

    • 8 min warmup
    • 8x 20secs @ 170% ftp and 7x 20secs @ rest
    • 8 min @ rest
    • 8x 20secs @ 170% ftp and 7x 20secs @ rest
    • 8 min @ rest
    • 8x 20secs @ 170% ftp and 7x 20secs @ rest
    • 8 min cooldown
