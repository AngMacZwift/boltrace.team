Wild Women Racing League

Event Information

The Wild Women Racing League is a new joint venture between the BRT Hellcatz, Velocity Vixen and the women of ZSUN.

  • Start Date: 5th January, 2019 UTC
  • End Date: 23rd February, 2019 UTC
  • Event Day & Time:
    • Saturday 6:55pm UTC
    • Saturday 1:30pm UTC
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Categories
    1. 3.7W/kg and OVER
    2. 3.2W/kg to 3.69W/kg
    3. 2.5W/kg to 3.19W/kg
    4. 2.49W/kg and UNDER
  • Race Rules
    • Women only.
    • Must be registered with ZwiftPower.
    • Riders going over 5W/kg at 95% of 20 minute power will be DQ'd without CEVAZ approval.
    • Riders going over 6W/kg for 5 minute power will be removed from results.
    • Heart rate monitors required for podium finishers (top 3 per category).
    • zPower is not eligible for podium in A or B categories.
    • Non-TT bikes permitted, unless TT race.
    • Powerups permitted.
    • Race Commissar decision will be final.
  • Individual Points
    • Points available depend on number of finishing riders; please refer to our points table for full details.
    • All participants finishing outside of the top 50 score 1 point.
    • All participants will get a bonus point for finishing.
    • Points scoring applied to each category.
  • Team Points
    • Each team will score the sum of their individual rider points.
    • Each team will also get a bonus point for each rider that finishes.
    • There will be separate podiums for teams in each category.
  • Points Rules
    • Points can only be earned in the correct category a participant races in.
    • Racing in an incorrect category will void any points accrued.
    • Points will carry over when a rider changes category during a current league.
    • Best 6 of 8 rounds count towards final standings.
  • Mid-League Upgrades
    • Riders going over category limits will be automatically upgraded in the official results.
    • When a rider has been automatically upgraded, they are expected to race in their new category for all future events.
