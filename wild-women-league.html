Wild Women Racing League

Event Information

The Wild Women Racing League is a new joint venture between the BRT Hellcatz, Velocity Vixen and the women of ZSUN and AHDR.

  • Start Date: 8th June, 2019 UTC
  • End Date: 27th July, 2019 UTC
  • Event Day & Time:
    • Saturday 12:30pm UTC
    • Saturday 9:55pm UTC
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Categories
    1. 3.7W/kg and OVER
    2. 3.2W/kg to 3.69W/kg
    3. 2.5W/kg to 3.19W/kg
    4. 2.49W/kg and UNDER
  • Race Rules
    • Women only. Shared accounts not eligible for official results.
    • Must be registered with ZwiftPower.
    • Riders going over 5W/kg at 95% of 20 minute power will be DQ'd without CEVAZ approval.
    • Riders going over 6W/kg for 5 minute power will be removed from results.
    • Heart rate monitors required for podium finishers, time penalty applied to place riders outside podium positions where possible.
    • zPower is not eligible for podium finishers, time penalty applied to place riders outside podium positions where possible.
    • Non-TT bikes permitted, unless TT race, and will result in disqualification.
    • Powerups permitted.
    • Race Commissar decision will be final.
  • Individual Points: Road/Climb/TT
    • Points use the 45-50+ column of the points table; please refer to our points table for full details.
    • All participants finishing outside of the top 50 score 1 point.
    • Points scoring applied to each category.
  • Individual Points: Climb
    • For each QOM, the top 6 will be awarded points (10, 7, 5, 3, 2, 1) with double points for the final climb. The best three climbers will be given a time bonus (-3, -2, -1 seconds) to their finish time.
  • Individual Points: Criterium
    • Finishing order will be determined by points earned from each sprint and the finish.
    • For each sprint segment, the top 6 will be awarded points (10, 7, 5, 3, 2, 1).
    • For the finish line, the top 6 will receive double points (20, 14, 10, 6, 4, 2).
  • Team Points
    • Each team will score the sum of their top 3 individual rider points.
    • There will be separate podiums for teams in each category.
  • Points Rules
    • Points can only be earned in the correct category a participant races in.
    • 50% of points will carry over when a rider changes category during a current league.
    • Best 6 of 8 rounds count towards final standings.
  • Mid-League Upgrades
    • Riders going over category limits will be automatically upgraded in the official results.
    • When a rider has been automatically upgraded, they are expected to race in their new category for the remainder of the series.
