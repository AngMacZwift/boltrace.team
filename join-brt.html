Guiding Principles

  • BRT are looking for riders willing to be part of a team from all categories and all corners of the world, who currently use a Powermeter or Smart Trainer.
  • BRT believe winning is only one element of racing on Zwift, and that the ladder to success should be lined with support, and that is the key to being a BRT member.
  • BRT have a vibrant community of over 250 members who are willing to help in your cause for glory, or times of difficulty.
  • BRT have two Facebook groups, one for all members (BRT), and one women’s specific (BRT Hellcatz).
  • BRT partake in the majority of high profile events on Zwift, and finish within the top three on a regular basis.
  • BRT run multiple events ranging from group rides, races, and race series.
  • BRT can offer you friendship through fitness.
  • BRT also has an IRL kit provided by Primal Europe.

If you would like to be part of the team, then please complete our membership form. Then click on the big orange Facebook thumb for BRT. And women can click the big purple Facebook thumb to also join the Hellcatz group below!
